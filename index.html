A bit about me

I grew up in Brighton, completed my first degree in Dundee and my Masters in London.

I have 4 children and a house full of pets.

After my first degree I secured a management training position with John Lewis but left after a year to start my Masters.

I worked in the City creating equity and bond trading systems for almost 8 years but reluctantly chose to leave the industry after the birth of my second child in 2001.

In order to balance the demands of a young family while keeping my brain active, I built on my property development experience and set up a kitchen design and build company that I could run from home.

Despite winning several awards and being featured in Elle Decoration, in 2019, when my youngest started secondary school I decided that I wanted to become a full-time developer again.
